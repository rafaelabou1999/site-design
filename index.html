Tips on how to create a creative design:

1.Keep it simple

The no. 1 and most important design tip for non-designers and beginner designers is to keep it simple. There’s nothing worse than an overwhelming design that is difficult to understand.

2.Use a cohersive color palette

Color palettes and color schemes are as important as the message you want to relay with your design. Choosing the perfect color combination though isn’t always easy.

3.Use Consistent Typography

Select two or three complementary fonts and stick to them throughout your design. Consistent typography helps create a unified and professional look.

4.Align Elements

Maintain visual harmony by aligning elements properly. Aligning text, images, and other design elements along a grid or common axis creates a sense of order and professionalism.
